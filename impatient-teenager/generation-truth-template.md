## SECTION 7: TECHNICAL SPECS

**Generation settings for consistent output quality.**

### Resolution & Aspect Ratio

| Shot Type | Resolution | Aspect Ratio | Orientation |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| Full body (T-pose, standing) | 1536 x 2816 | 9:16 | Portrait |
| Full body (wide room) | 2816 x 1536 | 16:9 | Landscape |
| Medium shot (waist up) | 2048 x 2048 | 1:1 | Square |
| Close-up portrait | 2048 x 2560 | 4:5 | Portrait |
| Bed/desk scenes | 2816 x 1536 | 16:9 | Landscape |

### Recommended Settings

- **Model:** Use photorealistic models (Flux, Midjourney v6+, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion XL)
- **Steps:** 30-50 steps for high detail
- **CFG Scale:** 5-7 (balance between prompt adherence and creativity)
- **Sampler:** DPM++ 2M Karras or Euler a (for SD-based models)
- **Seed:** Save seeds for successful generations to maintain consistency

### Lighting Notes

- **Primary light:** Phone screen glow (cool, bluish) illuminating face from below/front
- **Secondary light:** LED strips (purple `#9B59B6` or blue `#3498DB`) creating rim light on hair/shoulders
- **Ambient:** Dim, nocturnal feel — avoid bright, even lighting
- **Time of day:** Evening/night (LEDs on, curtains drawn) or overcast afternoon

### Color Grading

- **Dominant tones:** Cool (blue/purple LED cast)
- **Contrast:** Warm skin tones against cool background
- **Mood:** Dim, intimate, screen-lit, nocturnal
- **Post-processing:** Slight desaturation, cool temperature shift, subtle film grain
