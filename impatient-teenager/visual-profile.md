# Alex "The Scroller" Chen — Visual Profile

> **Purpose:** Practical reference for AI image generation prompts  
> **Character:** Alex Chen, 17, Gen Z digital native

> **Vibe:** Authentic, urban, always scrolling

## 1. Physical Appearance

### Age & Presence
- **Looks:** 17 years old (Gen Z through and through)
- **Build:** Slim, average height, spends too much time sitting
- **Energy:** Restless, thumb always moving, slight slouch from phone use

### Hair
- **Style:** Messy textured crop with faded sides, or shaggy medium-length with curtain bangs
- **Color:** Dark brown/black with subtle bleached tips or a single dyed streak (electric blue, purple, or silver)
- **Texture:** Slightly greasy (they skip washes when binging content), natural wave
- **Key detail:** Hair falls over forehead when leaning forward to scroll

### Clothing (What They Actually Wear)
- **Tops:** Oversized graphic tees (band merch, obscure anime, ironic slogans), hoodies with drawstrings hanging loose, flannel shirts worn open
- **Bottoms:** Baggy cargo pants, ripped jeans, or sweatpants (comfort > style, but still looks put-together)
- **Layers:** Unzipped hoodie over tee, or flannel tied around waist
- **Footwear:** Beat-up Converse, Vans, or New Balance sneakers (worn but clean)
- **Fit:** Everything slightly oversized, nothing tailored or stiff

### Accessories
- **Headphones:** Over-ear wireless (Sony XM4/5 or AirPods Max) — usually around neck, sometimes on ears
- **Phone:** iPhone 14/15 Pro with cracked screen protector, pop socket on back, case is worn/scratched (maybe a clear case yellowed with time, or a colorful abstract design)
- **Other:** Simple silver chain necklace, maybe a ring or two, smartwatch or fitness tracker (mostly ignored)
- **No:** No backpacks in bedroom, no formal accessories, no watches with leather straps

### Gender Presentation
- **Androgynous:** Clothing and styling should not read distinctly masculine or feminine
- **Neutral features:** Soft jawline, no visible facial hair, minimal or no makeup
- **Body language:** Relaxed, not posed, natural Gen Z slouch

## 2. Environment (Bedroom)

### Room Setup
- **Size:** Small to medium bedroom, feels lived-in
- **Layout:** Bed pushed against wall, desk in corner facing window or wall, floor space cluttered
- **Hangout zone:** Bed (primary scrolling spot) or desk chair (secondary)
- **Furniture:** IKEA-style minimal bed frame, basic desk, ergonomic gaming chair or worn office chair, maybe a beanbag in corner

### Lighting
- **Primary:** LED strip lights along ceiling perimeter or behind desk/bed headboard
- **LED Colors:** Purple, blue, pink, or RGB cycling (set to static color usually)
- **Secondary:** Natural light from window (often blocked by blackout curtains)
- **Ambient:** Desk lamp with adjustable arm, maybe a salt lamp or lava lamp for vibes
- **Mood:** Dim, screen-glow dominant, LEDs create colored rim light on Alex's face

### Decor (What's on the Walls)
- **Posters:** Band posters (hip-hop, indie, hyperpop), anime posters, movie prints (A24 style), aesthetic collage prints
- **Wall art:** Tape Polaroids in grid pattern, vinyl records mounted, fairy lights draped
- **Shelves:** Floating shelves with Funko Pops, succulents (barely alive), books stacked horizontally, random knick-knacks
- **No:** No framed family photos, no motivational posters, no generic stock art

### Tech Setup
- **Phone:** Always in hand or propped on desk
- **Laptop:** MacBook or gaming laptop on desk, stickers covering the lid, often open but ignored
- **Headphones:** On desk or around neck
- **Charging:** Messy cable situation, power strip visible, phone charging cable stretched across bed/desk
- **Speaker:** Small Bluetooth speaker (JBL Flip or similar) on desk

### Chaos Level
- **Desk:** Organized chaos — phone, laptop, snack wrappers, drink cans, notebooks with doodles, pens scattered
- **Bed:** Unmade, rumpled sheets, pillows in random positions, maybe a stuffed animal or two
- **Floor:** Clothes pile (not dirty, just "chair clothes"), shoes kicked off, maybe a skateboard
- **Overall:** Lived-in, not filthy, definitely not Instagram-clean

### Colors
- **Walls:** Neutral (white, light gray, or beige) — lets LEDs and posters pop
- **Accent colors:** LED-driven (purple/blue/pink), poster colors bleed into room
- **Textiles:** Black/gray bedding, maybe one colorful throw blanket
- **Floor:** Hardwood or cheap carpet, partially covered by rug (Persian-style or shag)

## 3. Typical Poses & Expressions

### Scrolling Pose (Default)
- **Body:** Slouched in chair or half-lying on bed, one leg up
- **Hands:** Phone held in right or left hand, thumb actively swiping
- **Arm position:** Elbow on knee or armrest, phone angled toward face
- **Gaze:** Eyes locked on screen, slight frown of concentration
- **Shoulders:** Rounded forward, classic "phone posture"

### Skeptical Expression (About to Scroll Away)
- **Eyebrows:** One raised slightly, or both furrowed
- **Mouth:** Slight grimace, lips pursed
- **Eyes:** Narrowed, looking down at phone
- **Head tilt:** Slight tilt back, like "really?"
- **Thumb:** Hovering over screen, ready to swipe

### Bored Expression (Dead Eyes)
- **Eyes:** Half-lidded, unfocused, glassy
- **Mouth:** Slightly open, relaxed, no expression
- **Face:** Completely neutral, almost blank
- **Posture:** More slouched, sinking into chair/bed
- **Energy:** Zero engagement, scrolling on autopilot

### Intrigued Expression (Leaning In)
- **Eyebrows:** Raised, especially inner brows
- **Eyes:** Wider, focused intently
- **Head:** Leaned forward, phone closer to face
- **Mouth:** Slightly parted, or small smile forming
- **Body:** Tensed slightly, more alert

### Genuinely Interested (Engaged)
- **Eyes:** Bright, focused, following action on screen
- **Mouth:** Small smile, or lips curved up at one corner
- **Posture:** Sitting up straighter, less slouched
- **Hands:** Both hands on phone now, or one hand supporting the other
- **Energy:** Present, actually watching

### Excited/Hooked (Reactive)
- **Eyes:** Wide, pupils slightly dilated
- **Mouth:** Open smile, or laugh starting to form
- **Head:** Thrown back slightly, or leaning in hard
- **Body:** Animated, might gesture with free hand
- **Energy:** High, fully captured

### Cringe/Disappointed (Face Palm)
- **Hand:** Covering part of face, or rubbing forehead
- **Eyes:** Closed or looking away from phone
- **Mouth:** Grimace, or pressed into thin line
- **Head:** Shaking slightly, or tilted down
- **Body:** Recoiling, creating distance from phone

## 6. Camera Angles

### Close-Up on Face + Phone (Reaction Shots)
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, phone visible in lower third of frame
- **Focus:** Sharp on face, phone screen slightly blurred (or screen glow visible)
- **Lighting:** LED rim light on hair/shoulders, screen glow on face
- **Use case:** Capturing micro-expressions, scroll reactions
- **Angle:** Slightly above eye level (like selfie angle but not quite)

### Over-Shoulder (Showing What They're Watching)
- **Framing:** Behind Alex's shoulder, phone screen visible in foreground
- **Focus:** Can show screen content (blurred if needed) or focus on Alex's reaction
- **Lighting:** Screen glow illuminates Alex's face from front
- **Use case:** Context for what they're scrolling, POV shots
- **Angle:** 45 degrees behind, slightly elevated

### Wide Room Shot (Environment Context)
- **Framing:** Full room or large portion, Alex small in frame (on bed or desk)
- **Focus:** Room details visible — posters, LEDs, chaos
- **Lighting:** LED strips visible, overall dim ambiance
- **Use case:** Establishing shots, showing lifestyle
- **Angle:** From doorway or corner, capturing depth

### Side Profile (Consistency Reference)
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders from side, phone in hand
- **Focus:** Sharp on profile, hair style visible, posture clear
- **Lighting:** Rim light from LEDs behind, face in partial shadow
- **Use case:** Reference sheets, consistent character modeling
- **Angle:** 90 degrees from front, level with face

### Best Angles for Authenticity
- **Slightly low angle:** Makes Alex look natural, not heroic
- **Handheld feel:** Slight imperfection, not perfectly stabilized
- **Natural distance:** Not too close (feels invasive), not too far (feels detached)
- **Avoid:** Perfectly centered, symmetrical shots (feels staged)

## 7. Image Generation Prompt Guidelines

### Key Phrases to Include (Consistency)
- "17-year-old Gen Z teenager, androgynous appearance, they/them"
- "messy textured dark hair with faded sides, subtle bleached tips"
- "oversized graphic tee, baggy cargo pants, beat-up Converse"
- "over-ear headphones around neck, iPhone with cracked screen protector"
- "slouched posture, thumb actively scrolling on phone"
- "bedroom with LED strip lights (purple/blue), dim screen-lit ambiance"
- "wall posters (anime, band merch), organized chaos, lived-in feel"
- "authentic Gen Z aesthetic, not staged, natural lighting from screen"
- "photorealistic, candid moment, captured mid-action"

### Phrases to Avoid (Feels Fake/Staged)
- ❌ "Professional photoshoot"
- ❌ "Studio lighting"
- ❌ "Perfectly posed"
- ❌ "Clean, minimalist room"
- ❌ "Smiling at camera"
- ❌ "Pristine clothing"
- ❌ "Stock photo aesthetic"
- ❌ "Corporate Memphis style"
- ❌ "Overly polished"
- ❌ "Generic teenager"

### Lighting Notes
- **Primary light source:** Phone screen glow on face (cool, bluish)
- **Secondary:** LED strips (purple/blue/pink, creates rim light)
- **Ambient:** Dim, nocturnal feel, shadows present
- **Avoid:** Bright, even lighting, no harsh overhead lights
- **Time of day:** Evening/night (LEDs on, curtains drawn) or overcast afternoon

### Style Notes
- **Photorealistic:** Should look like a candid photo, not illustration
- **Authentic:** Imperfect, lived-in, real textures (wrinkled sheets, worn clothes)
- **Documentary feel:** Like someone walked in and snapped a photo
- **Color grading:** Slightly desaturated, cool tones dominant, LED colors pop
- **Depth of field:** Shallow enough to focus on Alex, deep enough to show room context
- **Grain:** Slight film grain or digital noise okay (adds authenticity)
- **Avoid:** Airbrushed, plastic skin, perfect symmetry, stock photo smoothness

### Prompt Structure Template
```
[Subject description], [action/pose], [expression], [clothing], [accessories], 
[environment description], [lighting], [color palette], [style notes]

Example:
"17-year-old Gen Z teenager with messy dark hair and bleached tips, slouched in 
gaming chair scrolling iPhone, skeptical expression with raised eyebrow, wearing 
oversized band tee and baggy cargo pants, over-ear headphones around neck, bedroom 
with purple LED strips and anime posters on walls, dim screen-lit ambiance, cool 
tones with purple accent lighting, photorealistic candid shot, authentic not staged"
```
